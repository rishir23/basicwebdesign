Choose a Website Audience

As you can see, you have a great deal of freedom in selecting your website's focus. However, the topic you choose should be "rich" or detailed enough to produce content for the different pages it needs to show in the final site.

General

  • At least five additional pages (6 total), all styled using external CSS and made accessible to all audiences.
  • Pages should include internal links and use at least five additional pages.
  • Use headings, layout divisions, and semantic elements.
  • Design should be search-engine friendly.

Images

  • At least one image or graphic on every page, optimized for web with captions and alt text.

Lists

  • At least one list, styled with CSS classes and organized clearly.
  • Use classes to style and improve readability and appearance.
