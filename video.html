How to Embed a Video on a Web Page

Step 1: Copy the Embed Code

Go to your video platform (like YouTube), click "Share", and choose "Embed". Copy the iframe code.

Step 2: Paste it in Your HTML

In your HTML file, paste the iframe code where you want the video to appear.

Step 3: Save and Open

Save your HTML file and open it in a browser to make sure the video shows up correctly.
